About Us

No.10 Russell Street is our beautifully restored Georgian showhouse, gift store and coffee shop. Home to a fine range of fabrics, furnishings, furniture and lighting. With a wide range of designer fabrics available: Zoffany, Sanderson, Harlequin, Designers Guild, Romo and many more.We also have a wide range of unique gifts in our showroom.And can't visit us without trying our beautiful coffee shop full of homemade goodies, lunches and fair-trade tea and coffee.
